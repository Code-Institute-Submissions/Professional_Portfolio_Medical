Dhruv Parikh

MBBCh BSc FRCS(SN)
Mr Dhruv Parikh is a Neurosurgeon specialising in minimally invasive (endoscopic) pituitary and lateral skull base surgery. He currently holds a senior fellowship at the University Hospital of South Wales, Cardiff.

After completing his undergraduate medical studies at the University of Cardiff Mr Parikh received an MBBCh with honours and a specialist BSc in Neuroscience. He completed his postgraduate neurosurgical training in the West Midlands, gaining his completion of training in 2019. Mr Parikh specialises in state-of-the-art minimally invasive techniques, primarily to treat pituitary tumours.
