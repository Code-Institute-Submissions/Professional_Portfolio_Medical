Clinical Research

Credit: Mr Dhruv Parikh
Clinical research is essential to improve the treatment of patients with brain tumours. Mr Parikh is currently contributing to research projects and clinical trials that are tracking national trends and outcomes in pituitary surgery. In particular Mr Parikh's research aims to improve vision and reduce the time patients spend in hospital after surgery. If you would be interested in taking part in our active clinical research programs please let us know at your appointment. Click below to view research publications from Mr Parikh's research group.
